Professional Background

I am an accomplished graduate of the American University of Afghanistan with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, specializing in Data Science. My academic journey has equipped me with a robust foundation in computer science, mathematics, and statistical analysis.

Education Technology Officer As an Education Technology Officer at Lapis Group, I manage and optimize the Learning Passport Afghanistan (Dana) platform, along with Kankor exam resources and the Senf digital classroom feature. I use Apple technologies to remove barriers to learning, improve accessibility, and create engaging, structured educational content. My work also involves using Power BI to analyze data and guide important decisions.

Data Analyst – AUAF & Freiburg University of Education (DAAD) I served as a Data Analyst for AUAF and the Freiburg University of Education (DAAD), leading research on Afghanistan’s job market. I collected, cleaned, and analyzed data using Python, R, and web scraping to uncover trends in job categories, gender representation, and required skills.

IT Facilitator – Bayat Matter Innovation Hub As an IT Facilitator at the Bayat Matter Innovation Hub, I trained over 350 students in critical thinking, creativity, and technology skills through project-based learning. I also mentored students at the American University of Afghanistan, providing individualized academic guidance and fostering an engaging learning environment.
